Anti-Scam Awareness

Tip 1

Never click on suspicious links in emails or messages. Always verify the sender first.

Tip 2

Avoid sharing personal information like passwords or bank details via email.

Tip 3

Check for HTTPS in website URLs before entering sensitive information.

Tip 4

Be cautious of offers that seem too good to be true. Verify their authenticity.

Tip 5

Keep your devices updated with the latest security patches and antivirus software.

Tip 6

Always report phishing attempts to your email provider or IT team.
